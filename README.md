# AI Startups & AI in Markets

A guest talk for the DIEM Club (Decentralized Intelligence, Economics, and Markets Executive Team) at Emory University.

**Speaker:** Danilo Freire, Department of Data and Decision Sciences, Emory University
**Date:** April 2026
**Format:** ~20-25 minute talk, 20 content slides

## Topics

- Part 1: fundamentals to look for in AI startups (Y Combinator, unit economics, moats, case studies of winners and losers)
- Part 2: AI applied to financial markets (quant funds, LLMs in finance, alpha decay, retail AI tools)
